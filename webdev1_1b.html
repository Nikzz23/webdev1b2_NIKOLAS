Calamares recipe

Calamares



Calamares is a popular and beloved Filipino dish known for its delightful combination of tender, deep-fried squid rings encased in a crispy, golden-brown batter. Calamares has a mild and slightly sweet flavor from the squid itself.

Ingredients



  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 cup soda water
  • 2 cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 kg squid rings, cleanded and cut into rings
  • neutral oil, for frying

Steps



  1. Prepare for frying: Heat oil in a wok or heavy-bottomed pot to 350Â°F.
  2. Make the wet batter: Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl. While whisking continuously, slowly stream in soda water until a batter forms.
  3. Prepare the dry breading: Add panko breadcrumbs to a shallow bowl or baking sheet.
  4. Batter squid: Dip squid rings into the wet batter, then toss in breadcrumbs until evenly coated on all sides. Rest breaded squid on a tray and repeat with remaining squid.
  5. Fry squid: Working in batches, add breaded squid rings to the hot oil. Fry until crispy and golden brown, about 5 minutes. Transfer fried squid onto a metal rack or heatproof plate lined with paper towels to drain excess oil. Season with salt while warm. Serve with lemon wedges and dipping sauce of choice

Good for 6 people
